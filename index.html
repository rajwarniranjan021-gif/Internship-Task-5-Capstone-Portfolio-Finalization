💡 Key Findings

8 actionable insights derived from the end-to-end analysis.

💰

Revenue Drivers

Unit price explains 85% of variance in order revenue (r=0.855 correlation).

🏆

Top Category

Electronics leads on revenue; Sports shows strongest recent growth trajectory.

👤

Best Customers

Champions segment (RFM top tier) generates 3× revenue vs At-Risk customers.

📉

Retention Gap

Average Month-1 retention ~40% — strong acquisition, but re-engagement needed.

📢

Best Channel

Paid Search delivers the highest average revenue per lead (statistically validated).

🏷️

Discount Strategy

Discounted orders have significantly higher revenue (p<0.05) — discount high-value items, not low.

⭐

Consistent Service

ANOVA confirms satisfaction is statistically similar across all 5 regions — good ops consistency.

💳

Payment Behaviour

Payment method preference is independent of region — uniform checkout UX is appropriate.
